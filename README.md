# FYP
Expected Attendance Statistics

Daily Attendance Records:

Date of attendance.
Total number of students present and absent.
A list of students with their status (present or absent).

Monthly or Weekly Attendance Summary:

Total attendance percentage for each student over a month or week.
Average attendance percentage for the class.
Number of classes held and number of classes attended by each student.

Student-Specific Attendance:

Attendance history for individual students.
Trends over time (e.g., improving or declining attendance).
Geofencing Compliance (if applicable):

Records of students who were present at the designated location during attendance-taking.

Overall Class Attendance:

Percentage of attendance for the entire class.
Comparison of attendance across different courses.
